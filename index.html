About Vision Fund

A startup without a funding source will flounder under the weight of its own debt. Funding is the fuel on which a startup runs. A startup can take different avenues to attain funding, and more than one option can be used. The chosen funding will depend on the startup’s desire to be in debt, how solvent the startup owners are at the time the startup is founded and the amount of money a startup will need to launch and maintain itself through a variety of events. Startup owners, just like any other business owners, need additional funding to enhance, expand, grow, or start their startup. New ventures or startups do not just have to deal with a variety of processes like gaining traction, ideation, expansion, etc. All these processes require money, and that money should be something that should be provided to them at the right time so that the growth of the startup is not halted. This is why startups should opt for ‘EDC 100 Crore Vision Fund.’ After acquiring the right amount of funds, we also help the business owners to ensure that the money raised goes to the right place and is used for the right purpose. A great idea and a strong will to succeed are just not enough in today’s generation. If you’re an entrepreneur who’s looking to grow and scale, you should look into funding your startup business through EDC 100 Crore Vision Fund. We provide the token amount to startups from INR 5Lakh to INR 1 Crore.
